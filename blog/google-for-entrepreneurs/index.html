Product Updates

 

Hangouts in Google Calendar events

 

Users with an active Google+ profile can add a Google+ Hangout to calendar events. Invited guests can join the hangout by clicking on “Join hangout” on the event details page. These hangouts will respect any restrictions in the administrator control panel. Learn more.

Discussions are now available for Spreadsheets and Sites

 

Google Spreadsheets and Sites users now have the ability to collaborate more efficiently with discussions and comments. This brings powerful features like the ability to +mention users to add them to the discussion and responding to comments via email. Learn more about discussions in Spreadsheets and Sites.


IE8 deprecation details

 

As you may have seen in a recent Google blog post, support for Microsoft Internet Explorer 8 (IE8) will be deprecated beginning November 15, 2012.  Starting this week, IE8 users will begin seeing an in-product notification that their browser will soon be unsupported. If you have users accessing Google Apps from IE8, we want to ensure your users are ready to have the best Google Apps experience.  We strongly recommend that our customers implement a dual-browser strategy by deploying Google Chrome for Business for Google Apps use and leaving IE8 for legacy web apps. Contact us for more information.
