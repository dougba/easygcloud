EasyG Apps.com Becomes a Google Apps Premier Partner

June 6, 2014 -- EasyG Apps.com announced it has become a Premier Partner of the Google Apps for Work™ suite of communication and collaboration tools.

This new designation from Google enables customers to more easily assess a partner’s expertise advising on and deploying Google services. EasyG Apps.com was promoted from Authorized to Premier Partner based on their expertise and success in helping customers deploy and use Google Apps. EasyG Apps.com helps businesses evolve from premise based legacy software to web based cloud solutions that save time and money while increasing sales and profits, helping teams work smarter and focusing on people instead of technology.

 

"Most businesses can increase sales and reduce costs by refining their business process. Selecting the right technologies from the right vendors differentiates the most competitive businesses. Whether you're planning a startup or managing hundreds of employees, we will deliver. We'll analyze the best way to move your business process to the cloud, deploy solutions using best practices and help you grow your business." said Doug Baumwall at EasyG Apps.com. "We’re pleased Google recognizes us as a Premier Partner and look forward to helping more people with modern solutions like Google Apps for Work.”

 

Google Apps brings simple, powerful communication and collaboration tools to organizations of any size – all hosted by Google to streamline setup, minimize maintenance, and reduce costs. With Gmail, Google Calendar, and integrated Instant Messaging, users can stay connected and work together easily. Using Google Docs and Google Sites, which include word processing, spreadsheet and presentation tools, they can share files and collaborate in real-time, keeping versions organized and available wherever and whenever users work.

 

The Google Apps Partner program includes companies globally that sell, service and customize Google Apps for Business for their customers. As a part of the Partner program, EasyG Apps.com receives training, support and deployment tools from Google. To learn more about Google’s business software, please visit EasyG Apps.com or call (786) 276-7900.
