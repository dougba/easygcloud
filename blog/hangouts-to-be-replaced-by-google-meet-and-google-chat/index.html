Hangouts to be replaced by Google Meet and Google Chat

Whether you love it or hate it, Google Hangouts has helped millions of people connect better.

Last year, Google Meet was released and today, Google Chat is going live.

Meet and Chat will eventually replace the original Google Hangouts.

Both are new, improved, specialized and optimized for a much better experience.

Your admin may need to turn on Google Meet for you, and you can try Google Chat

here: https://get.google.com/chat/
